Beef Bowl

Felu - Fit by cooking

Description

This is a low calorie, high protein recipe for a beef bowl. It is created by Felu - Fit by cooking and used for this assignment.

Ingredients

Bowl
  • ground beef 180g
  • potatoes 250g
  • red bell pepper 150g
  • onion 50g
  • salt, pepper
  • 1/4 tsp each garlic powder, chili powder
Sauce
  • cottage cheese 100g
  • tomato sauce 50g
  • evaporated milk 50g
  • salt, pepper
  • 1/2 tsp oregano

Steps

  1. Peel potatoes and then cut them into cubes
  2. Put the potatoes in a microwave safe container and microwave for 5 minutes at 600W
  3. Cut red bell pepper nd onion into smaller pieces
  4. Put all sauce ingredients into a blender and mix for 60 seconds
  5. Heat up a pan to medium heat and add 4g of oil
  6. Fry the potatoes for 5 minutes until crispy
  7. Add a bit more oil and then the vegetables
  8. Keep frying for another 3-4 minutes
  9. Add another bit more oil and add in the beef
  10. Spread the beef so as much as possible gets in contact with the pan
  11. Add salt, pepper, garlic powder, and chili powder
  12. After 1 minute mix everything and keep frying for another minute
  13. Remove the pan from the heat and add sauce on top
  14. Mix everything once again and done!
